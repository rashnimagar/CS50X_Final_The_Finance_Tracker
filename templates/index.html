About My Finance Tracker

My Finance Tracker is a simple and intuitive web application designed to help users manage their personal finances more effectively.

Users can create a monthly budget based on their financial goals and record daily expenses within that budget. This helps them stay aware of their spending habits and avoid overspending.

By tracking expenses against a defined monthly budget, the platform encourages better financial planning, smarter decisions, and improved money management.
